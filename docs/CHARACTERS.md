## üî≠ RECRUIT
**"The Rookie with Fire"**

### Personality
Eager, curious, and hungry to learn. Ask a million questions because they want to get it right. Energy is high, confidence is low‚Äîbut potential is off the charts.

### Voice
- Uses phrases like "Just curious..." and "Wait, why do we..."
- Gets excited about small wins
- Often starts sentences with "I was thinking..."
- Self-deprecating humor ("Probably a dumb question, but...")

### Key Traits
| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Curiosity** | Learns by asking, not guessing |
| **Resilience** | Bounces back from rejection quickly |
| **Hunger** | Wants to prove themselves |
| **Adaptability** | Takes feedback well |

### What They're Known For
- Shadowing agents and taking furious notes
- Asking "why?" about everything
- First to volunteer for demos
- Remembering names and details

### Motivation
"Show me how to be great."

## üéØ HUNTER
**"The Cold Call Machine"**

### Personality
Relentless, bold, and never takes no personally. They thrive on the hustle‚Äîfinding opportunities where others see dead ends. Fast-talking but sincere.

### Voice
- Energetic, punchy sentences
- Uses numbers and metrics ("I hit 50 calls today")
- Direct and action-oriented
- Talks in present tense ("I'm crushing it")

### Key Traits
| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Fearlessness** | Pitches anyone, anytime |
| **Speed** | Works fast, moves fast |
| **Persistence** | Follows up until answer |
| **Pattern Recognition** | Spots lead signals instantly |

### What They're Known For
- Always has 50 tabs open
- Knows every prospect's LinkedIn
- Finds "warm leads" in cold pools
- First in, first out every day

### Motivation
"Every no is one step closer to yes."

## üéØ CLOSER
**"The Deal Whisperer"**

### Personality
Calm under pressure, surgical with words. They read the room before speaking. When they talk, people listen‚Äînot because they're loud, but because they're strategic.

### Voice
- Deliberate pacing, pauses for effect
- Uses silence as a tool
- "Let me ask you this..." framing
- Confident but not arrogant

### Key Traits
| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Emotional Intelligence** | Reads buyer signals perfectly |
| **Patience** | Never rushes the deal |
| **Negotiation** | Gets better terms, every time |
| **Closing Instinct** | Knows EXACTLY when to ask |

### What They're Known For
- Turning "I'll think about it" into signed contracts
- Knowing the perfect objection handler for every stall
- Never discounting‚Äîinstead, adding value
- The handshake moment

### Motivation
"The deal isn't closed until money moves."

## üå± CULTIVATOR
**"The Relationship Builder"**

### Personality

| **Patience** | Nurtures long-term relationships |
| **Trustworthiness** | Never oversells |
| **Consistency** | Always shows up, always follows through |

### What They're Known For
- Getting referrals from closed deals
- Clients who stay for years
- Remembering details from months ago
- Demo presentations that feel like conversations

### Motivation
"People don't buy from companies‚Äîthey buy from people."

## Character Voice Examples

### Same Situation, Different Roles

**The Scenario:** Prospect says "Your price is too high."

| Role | Response |
|------|----------|
| Recruit | "Um, let me check if we can do a discount... Actually, wait, what's your budget?" |
| Hunter | "Tell me what you're paying now‚ÄîI bet I'll show you ROI that blows it away." |
| Closer | *[Silence]* "Let me ask you this... if price was perfect, would we sign today?" |
| Cultivator | "I hear you. A lot of our clients felt the same way at first. Can I share how they justified it?" |
| Champion | "Totally fair. Let me see if there's a way to structure this differently for you..." |
| Senior Agent | "Price objection‚Äîclassic. Here's how I've handled this before..." |
| Agency Lead | "That's an important concern. Let's make sure you're getting exactly what you need first." |

## Using This System

### For AI Coaching
When SellSig provides real-time coaching during a call:
1. Detect which role the agent is in
2. Match coaching tone to their character archetype
3. Use their preferred language style

### For Onboarding
New recruits get a "character sheet" showing their archetype, traits, and voice.

### For Team Building
Mix complementary personalities (Hunter + Cultivator = killer combo).
